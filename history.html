J.Cole History

2014 Forest Hills Drive is the third studio album
by American hip hop recording artist J. Cole. It
was released on December 9, 2014, by Dreamville
Records, ByStorm Entertainment, Roc Nation and
Columbia Records. [4] Recording sessions took
place over the whole year, while the production on
the album was primarily handled by Cole himself,
along with several others such as Illmind, Vinylz,
Phonix Beats and Willie B. It was announced three
weeks before its release and had very little marketing,
with no singles or promotion taking place prior to its release.
The album was supported by four singles: "Apparently",
"Wet Dreamz", "No Role Modelz" and "Love Yourz".
